Role : Cloud & Data Engineering

Designation : Data Engineer
Preferred work exp : 5 - 10 years

Role Description:

Be the key liaison between the client team and the DataOps team. Elicit and drive use cases and related requirements based on detailed data analysis for client(s). Accurately document the requirements and assist in translating them into the existing technology solution. Use your strong communication and data analysis skills, attention to detail and process acumen to achieve the delivery goal of 100% quality along with the DataOps team.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Own the platform development process from inception to production deployment, including project scheduling, design, implementation and coordination with other team members.
  • Design and develop new components of the big data platform.
  • Work closely with Product Management and Data Scientist teams to define and refine the big data platform to achieve company product and business objectives.
  • Participate in critical analysis of test results and deliver solutions to problem areas.
  • Research and experiment with emerging technologies and tools related to big data.

Qualifications/Certifications

  • B.E/ B. Tech/ M.S/ M.C.A

What are we looking for?

  • 5 to 7 years of experience in software development

Other information

  • Willingness to attend meetings suitable to client specific timezones, as required.
